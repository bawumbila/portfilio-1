WAVe Music Playlist App. (CSS, Express, MongoDB, Mongoose, CORS, NodeJS, Morgan)

Helps you create a playlist from your favorite tracks. Select a track from the pre-populated tracks that populates current songs on the charts or enter your favorite songs in the "Track Name" and "Artist Name" fields. Your song is displayed in your playlist along with the albumart if available. You can click the play button which will take you to LastFM to play your selected song and watch music video if available. You can delete songs from your list by clicking the "Delete" button beside each song.
